Research Projects

Featured Research Projects

The MINT project is developing a modeling environment which will significantly reduce the time needed to develop new integrated models while ensuring their utility and accuracy.

The Intelligent Systems for Geosciences (IS-GEO) project represents an emerging community of interdisciplinary researchers producing fundamental new capabilities for understanding Earth systems and how the application of IS technologies can cultivate key new developments in both fields.

The Planet Texas 2050 project seeks to make Texas a habitable, sustainable, and equitable environment for human settlement by 2050. It is an eight-year sprint to find solutions that will make our communities more resilient and better prepared for the future.
